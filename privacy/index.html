Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by Corevo, including TinyCompress and OneBetter Fixed.

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data on external servers.

On-Device Processing

TinyCompress performs all image compression entirely on your device. No images are uploaded or processed remotely.

App data such as settings or user-created entries are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information.

Notifications

If you enable notifications (for example, reminders in OneBetter Fixed), they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not use third-party analytics, tracking systems, or advertising frameworks.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.
